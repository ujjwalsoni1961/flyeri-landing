Privacy Policy

Flyeri is built privacy-first. You control what you share, and you can block, report, or delete your account at any time. A full policy will be published here before launch.

Flyeri helps travellers find trusted companions on the same flight. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

Account details (name, email, phone), trip details you enter, and boarding-pass images you upload for ticket verification. We use Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage) to operate the service.

Advertising

We show ads through Google AdSense / AdMob. Depending on your consent and region, advertising partners may use identifiers and cookies to provide personalised or non-personalised ads. In the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland we ask for your consent before using data for personalised advertising, and on iOS we request tracking permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

Your choices

You can update or delete your account at any time from within the app. Deleting your account removes your profile, trips, and uploaded files, subject to limited legal retention.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Flyeri team at the support email hello@flyeri.app
